Grief Counseling

Grieving is as much a part of our lives as living and dying. Many of us, however, won’t seek help following a devastating loss. Hosparus is so committed to grief counseling that we offer services at no cost for up to 13 months after the death of a loved one. This includes pre-death counseling for anticipated deaths.
